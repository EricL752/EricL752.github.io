The Last day of March in 2023

This is my first blog to comment my coding career as a tester. First of all, I want to thank my friend——config-Li, thanks to him this blog can be built and I can start my writing career as a 26-year-old Chinglish boy.
At the beginning, I was just looking forward to becomming a coder because of the salary. Of course, the programmer has an expected money career, but as a test engineer in the automobile industry, our job requires us to produce a safer and more reliable software. So I did a lot of testing according to the documentation of the International Organization of Standardization. Although I didn’t study in software during my college years, but I started to think how to use code ability to work efficiently.
I did it. I coded in some projects, I felt very satisfied, I also went through some hardships and challenges, but I know that I can achieve something and benefit from it, because many predecessors have been built. It seems that I recite a famous poams, the poams is very beautiful, but I just copy it instead of writing it, like a CV-engineer(CTRL+C & CTRL+V). I sure, I could not be a great software engineer, but I hope, I can build my “tree” in the field of testing.
